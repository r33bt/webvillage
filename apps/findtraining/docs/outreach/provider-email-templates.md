# FindTraining.com — Provider Outreach Email Templates

Cold outreach to HRDF-registered training providers.
All three templates reference the free listing already in the directory and push to /founding.

---

## Template 1 — Cold intro

**Subject:** Your company is already listed on FindTraining.com

Hi [First Name],

I wanted to let you know — [Company Name] already has a free listing on FindTraining.com. We pulled HRDF-registered providers into our directory so HR managers can start finding you.

The free listing shows your name and HRDF number. Founding members (first 50 only) get a full profile with direct contact, up to 5 course listings, lead notifications, and an HRDF-verified badge — all at RM 100/mo, locked for life.

After the founding slots close, it goes to RM 300/mo.

Worth a look if you want to start getting inbound leads before we launch publicly:
https://findtraining.com/founding

Happy to answer any questions.

[Your Name]
FindTraining.com

## Usage notes

- Replace `[First Name]`, `[Company Name]`, and `[Your Name]` before sending.
- Template 2: fill in `[X]` with current registered HR manager count (check with team).
- Sequence: send Template 1, wait 5–7 days, send Template 2. Template 3 is for anyone who visited /founding or /providers but did not sign up.
- Sending tool: Saleshandy (Lifetime Pro). Use a GWS mailbox on findtraining.com domain.
- Keep daily sends under 50/mailbox to protect deliverability during warmup.
